Publications

A selected list of published books in the Lou Shaokun Jingfang series (娄绍昆经方系列丛书), including simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, and English editions. The sequence below reflects a structured learning path: from foundational narrative, to case-based application, and finally to advanced textual study of the Shanghan Lun.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-jingfang-case-studies.jpg" alt="The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun (English edition)" />

The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun

English edition · Purple Cloud Press (USA) · Published November 2024

This English-language volume presents clinical case studies and major medical treatises by Dr. Lou Shaokun. It is widely regarded as a valuable teaching resource for students and clinicians of classical formula medicine.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-zhongyi-rensheng.jpg" alt="Zhongyi Rensheng - A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine" />

Zhongyi Rensheng
A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine

Simplified & Traditional Chinese editions · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine / Wanderer Press (Taiwan)

First published in 2012, this foundational work documents Dr. Lou Shaokun’s lifelong engagement with Jingfang medicine. Total circulation has exceeded 100,000 copies.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-jingfang-case-cn.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun Jingfang Case Records and Medical Notes" />

Lou Shaokun Jingfang Case Records & Medical Notes

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · 2019

Extensive clinical case records illustrating formula–pattern diagnosis and long-term treatment observation, serving as a bridge to advanced study.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-one-formula-one-needle.jpg" alt="One Formula, One Needle: Understanding Shanghan Lun" />

One Formula, One Needle: Understanding the Shanghan Lun

Simplified Chinese · Published 2021
English translation in progress (expected March 2026)

Integrates classical herbal formulas with acupuncture practice, offering a structured learning pathway for practitioners at different stages.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-kangzhiben-shanghanlun.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghan Lun" />

Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghan Lun

Simplified Chinese · Published 2023
Traditional Chinese edition forthcoming (Wanderer Press, Taiwan)

A comprehensive commentary synthesizing decades of textual research and clinical experience, widely influential in the classical-formula community.
