The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun

English edition · Purple Cloud Press (USA) · Published November 2024

This English-language volume presents clinical case studies and major medical treatises by Dr. Lou Shaokun. It has been recommended by Western practitioners as a valuable teaching resource for students and clinicians.

A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine: One Master Physician’s Long Engagement with Jingfang

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine (China) · First published June 2012
Traditional Chinese · Wanderer Press (Taiwan) · First published 2016
Revised & expanded editions published later · Total sales across editions: 100,000+

A foundational work documenting Dr. Lou’s clinical journey and the intellectual framework of Jingfang practice. An English edition has been translated and refined and is being prepared for U.S. publication.

Zhongyi Rensheng (中医人生)

4 published editions (simplified/traditional; revised/expanded)

Recommended starting point for learners entering the Lou Shaokun Jingfang pathway.
