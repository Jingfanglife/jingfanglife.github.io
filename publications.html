Publications

A selected list of published books in the Lou Shaokun Jingfang series (娄绍昆经方系列丛书), including simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, and English editions. The sequence reflects a structured learning path from foundational narrative, to case-based clinical application, and finally to advanced textual study of the Shanghan Lun.

< img class="pub-cover" src="/assets/images/book-jingfang-case-studies.jpg" alt="The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun">

The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun

English edition · Purple Cloud Press (USA) · Published November 2024

This English-language volume presents detailed clinical case studies and major medical treatises by Dr. Lou Shaokun. It is widely regarded as a valuable teaching resource for Western practitioners of classical formula medicine.

< img class="pub-cover" src="/assets/images/book-zhongyi-rensheng.jpg" alt="Zhongyi Rensheng – A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine">

Zhongyi Rensheng
A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine

Simplified & Traditional Chinese editions · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine / Wanderer Press (Taiwan)

First published in 2012, this foundational work documents Dr. Lou Shaokun’s lifelong engagement with Jingfang medicine. Across multiple editions, total circulation has exceeded 100,000 copies. An English edition is currently in preparation.

< img class="pub-cover" src="/assets/images/book-jingfang-case-cn.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun Jingfang Case Records and Medical Notes">

Lou Shaokun Jingfang Case Records & Medical Notes

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published 2019

These volumes present extensive clinical case records, serving as a bridge between foundational narrative learning and advanced formula-textual study within the Jingfang system.

< img class="pub-cover" src="/assets/images/book-one-formula-one-needle.jpg" alt="One Formula, One Needle – Understanding the Shanghan Lun">

One Formula, One Needle
Understanding the Shanghan Lun

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published 2021
English translation in progress (expected completion: March 2026)

Building on decades of clinical experience, this work integrates classical herbal formulas with acupuncture practice, offering a structured pathway from beginners to experienced clinicians.

< img class="pub-cover" src="/assets/images/book-kangzhiben-shanghanlun.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghanlun">

Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghanlun

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published December 2023

This scholarly commentary is the first systematic study of the Shanghan Lun based on the Kangzhiben edition, sparking a new nationwide wave of classical formula study.
