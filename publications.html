Publications

A selected list of published books in the Lou Shaokun Jingfang series (娄绍昆经方系列丛书), including simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, and English editions. The sequence below reflects a structured learning path: from foundational narrative, to case-based application, and finally to advanced textual study of the Shanghan Lun.

The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun

English edition · Purple Cloud Press (USA) · Published November 2024

This English-language volume presents clinical case studies and major medical treatises by Dr. Lou Shaokun. It has been recognized by Western practitioners as the first English work to provide detailed abdominal diagnosis records throughout the treatment process, and is widely regarded as a valuable teaching resource for students and clinicians of classical formula medicine.

Zhongyi Rensheng
A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine

Simplified & Traditional Chinese editions · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine / Wanderer Press (Taiwan)

First published in 2012, this foundational work documents Dr. Lou Shaokun’s lifelong engagement with Jingfang medicine. Across multiple editions in simplified and traditional Chinese, total circulation has exceeded 100,000 copies. An English translation is currently in preparation for publication.

Lou Shaokun Jingfang Case Records & Medical Notes

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published 2019

These volumes present extensive clinical case records illustrating formula–pattern diagnosis and long-term treatment observation. They serve as a core bridge between narrative foundations and advanced textual study within the Jingfang learning sequence.

One Formula, One Needle: Understanding the Shanghan Lun

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published 2021
English translation in progress (expected completion: March 2026)

Building upon decades of clinical experience, this work integrates classical herbal formulas with acupuncture practice. It offers a structured pathway for learners at different stages, from beginners to experienced clinicians, emphasizing practical application rooted in long-term clinical verification.

Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghan Lun

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · Published 2023
Traditional Chinese edition forthcoming (Taiwan, Wanderer Press)

This comprehensive commentary represents the culmination of decades of textual research and clinical practice. Since its release, the book has gone through multiple printings and has sparked renewed nationwide study of the Shanghan Lun within the classical-formula community.
