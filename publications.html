Publications

A selected list of published books in the Lou Shaokun Jingfang series (娄绍昆经方系列丛书), including simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, and English editions. The sequence below reflects a structured learning path: from foundational narrative and clinical worldview, to case-based application, then to advanced formula-and-text study of the Shanghan Lun.

The Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises of Dr. Lou Shaokun

English edition · Purple Cloud Press (USA) · Published November 2024

This English-language volume presents clinical case studies and major medical treatises by Dr. Lou Shaokun. Practitioners in the West have highlighted its detailed abdominal diagnosis records and its value as a teaching resource for students and clinicians of classical formula medicine.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-zhongyi-rensheng.jpg" alt="Zhongyi Rensheng (A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine) editions" />

A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine: One Master Physician’s Long Engagement with Jingfang

Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine (China) · First published June 2012
Traditional Chinese · Wanderer Press (Taiwan) · First published 2016
Revised & expanded editions (simplified + traditional) later published · Total sales (all Chinese editions) exceed 100,000 copies

A foundational work that presents the life-long clinical path and intellectual formation of Dr. Lou Shaokun, and the practical development of Jingfang medicine through decades of real-world practice. The book has been reprinted many times across editions and has become a widely read entry point for learners of classical formulas.

An English translation has been completed and refined through editorial work and is being prepared for publication to introduce classical formula medicine to a broader Western audience.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-jingfang-case-cn.jpg" alt="Two-volume set: Lou Shaokun on Classical Formulas and Clinical Case Records & Medical Notes (Simplified Chinese)" />

Lou Shaokun on Classical Formulas
Clinical Case Records & Medical Notes in Jingfang Medicine

Two-volume set · Simplified Chinese · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine · First published March 2019
Combined circulation exceeds 36,000 copies

This combined publication consists of two closely related volumes: Lou Shaokun on Classical Formulas and Clinical Case Records & Medical Notes in Jingfang Medicine. Together, they present Dr. Lou Shaokun’s mature clinical thinking, formula-based reasoning, and detailed case documentation developed over decades of practice.

The first volume focuses on the conceptual framework of Jingfang medicine, while the second records real clinical cases and reflective medical notes, demonstrating how classical formulas are applied, adjusted, and verified in practice. These works form a crucial bridge between foundational study and advanced clinical application.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-one-formula-one-needle.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun: One Formula, One Needle (Simplified Chinese cover)" />

One Formula, One Needle: Understanding Shanghan Lun Through Formulas and Acupuncture

Simplified Chinese edition · Published 2021 · English translation in progress (expected completion March 2026)

Building upon the clinical worldview presented in A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine, this work integrates decades of Jingfang practice with acupuncture experience and case-based insights. It discusses over 50 formula-patterns and many practical clinical points, offering a structured bridge from theory to application.

< img class="wide-img" src="/assets/images/book-kangzhiben-shanghanlun.jpg" alt="Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghanlun cover" />

Lou Shaokun’s Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghan Lun

Published December 2023 · China Press of Traditional Chinese Medicine (Beijing)
Traditional Chinese edition (Wanderer Press, Taiwan): forthcoming

A major scholarly commentary that annotates and studies the Shanghan Lun through the Kangzhiben edition. The work has sparked widespread discussion in China’s classical-formula community and has rapidly entered multiple printings.

This volume represents an advanced stage of study for readers who have built a foundation through narrative understanding, clinical cases, and formula-pattern practice, and now seek deeper textual and methodological clarity.

Suggested Learning Path

A practical, layered pathway for beginners and clinicians

  • A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine — foundation and clinical worldview
  • Lou Shaokun on Classical Formulas — core framework and reasoning
  • Clinical Case Records & Medical Notes — real-case verification and method
  • One Formula, One Needle — integrated formula + acupuncture approach
  • Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghan Lun — advanced text-based mastery
