Academic research, international lectures, and scholarly engagement in classical Jingfang (经方) medicine.

Academic Focus

Lou Shenshan’s academic work focuses on the theoretical structure, clinical methodology, and contemporary transmission of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine. Her research integrates close textual study with long-term clinical verification.

Rather than treating Jingfang as isolated prescriptions, her work emphasizes internal diagnostic logic, formula classification, and reproducible clinical reasoning.
