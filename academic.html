Academic

This section presents the academic framework, intellectual lineage, and structured learning pathway of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine, based on the lifelong clinical work of Dr. Lou Shaokun.

Academic Orientation

The Lou Shaokun Jingfang system emphasizes a return to classical medical texts through rigorous clinical verification, formula-pattern (方证) reasoning, and long-term case observation. It integrates herbal medicine, acupuncture, and textual scholarship into a unified clinical methodology.

Core Academic Focus

  • Classical formula-pattern (方证) diagnosis and clinical application
  • Comparative study of different textual editions of the Shanghan Lun
  • Integration of herbal formulas and acupuncture in complex cases
  • Abdominal diagnosis (腹诊) and physical examination in Jingfang medicine
  • Transmission of clinical experience through structured case studies

Textual Lineage

Special emphasis is placed on the study of the Kangzhiben edition of the Shanghan Lun, regarded as closer to the original structure of Shanghan Zabing Lun. Comparative reading with the Song edition is used to clarify textual evolution and clinical logic.

Learning Pathway

The recommended progression for learners follows a step-by-step structure:

  • A Life Devoted to Chinese Medicine — establishing foundational clinical thinking
  • Jingfang Case Studies and Medical Treatises — developing case-based reasoning
  • One Formula, One Needle: Understanding the Shanghan Lun — integrating formulas and acupuncture
  • Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghanlun — advanced textual and clinical synthesis
