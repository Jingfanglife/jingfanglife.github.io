Academic

This section presents the academic framework, intellectual lineage, and structured learning pathway of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine based on the lifelong clinical work of Dr. Lou Shaokun.

Academic Orientation

The Lou Shaokun Jingfang system emphasizes a return to classical medical texts through rigorous clinical verification, formula-pattern (方证) reasoning, and long-term case observation. It integrates herbal medicine, acupuncture, and textual scholarship into a unified clinical methodology.

Core Academic Focus

  • Classical formula-pattern (方证) diagnosis and clinical application
  • Comparative study of different textual editions of the Shanghan Lun
  • Integration of herbal formulas and acupuncture in complex cases
  • Transmission of clinical reasoning through case records and teaching texts
  • English translation and academic contextualization for Western readers

Structured Learning Pathway

Based on decades of teaching and clinical experience, the recommended progression for learners is:

  • Zhongyi Rensheng (中医人生) — establishing worldview, learning attitude, and foundational understanding of Jingfang medicine
  • Jingfang Case Records & Clinical Notes (经方医案医话) — developing pattern recognition through real clinical cases
  • One Formula, One Needle: Understanding Shanghan Lun (一方一针解伤寒) — structured entry into classical formulas with integrated acupuncture experience
  • Commentary on the Kangzhiben Shanghanlun (讲康治本《伤寒论》) — advanced, text-based mastery of the Treatise on Cold Damage

International Academic Engagement

Through publications, translations, and teaching activities, this body of work has been introduced to practitioners and students in Europe, North America, and East Asia, contributing to the global understanding of classical Chinese medicine and Jingfang practice.
