Academic

Academic research, international lectures, and scholarly engagement in classical Jingfang (经方) medicine.

Academic Focus

Lou Shenshan’s academic work focuses on the theoretical structure, clinical methodology, and contemporary transmission of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine. Her research emphasizes close textual study of classical sources combined with long-term clinical verification.

Rather than approaching Jingfang as isolated prescriptions, her work highlights its internal diagnostic logic, formula classification, and reproducible clinical reasoning.

Academic Orientation

Lou Shenshan’s academic orientation emphasizes methodological clarity, historical accuracy, and long-term clinical validation. Her work aims to support the sustainable transmission of Jingfang medicine within international academic and professional discourse.
