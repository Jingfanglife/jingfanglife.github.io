Academic Focus

My academic work centers on the study, interpretation, and clinical transmission of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine, with an emphasis on clear theoretical structure and reproducible clinical reasoning.

Rather than treating Jingfang as isolated prescriptions, I focus on its internal diagnostic logic, formula classification, and clinically testable decision pathways—linking classical source study with long-term clinical verification.

The Jingfang System

My approach highlights Jingfang as a coherent medical system: pattern differentiation, formula families, and structured treatment strategy. This includes clinical methodology (diagnostic structure and follow-through), as well as careful translation and cross-cultural explanation for international audiences.
