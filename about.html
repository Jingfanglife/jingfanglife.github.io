About Lou Shenshan

Lou Shenshan is a practitioner, researcher, and translator specializing in classical Jingfang (经方) medicine. Her work focuses on academic interpretation, clinical transmission, and international dissemination of traditional Chinese medical knowledge.

She builds upon the clinical and scholarly legacy of her father, Dr. Lou Shao-kun, a widely recognized authority in Jingfang medicine, while developing an independent body of work oriented toward global medical education and cross-cultural scholarship.
