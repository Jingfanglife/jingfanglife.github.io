About Lou Shenshan

Lou Shenshan is a practitioner, researcher, and translator specializing in classical Jingfang (经方) medicine. Her work focuses on the academic interpretation, clinical transmission, and international dissemination of traditional Chinese medical knowledge, particularly within the Jingfang lineage.

She builds upon the clinical and scholarly legacy of her father, Dr. Lou Shao-kun, a widely recognized authority in Jingfang medicine in China, while developing an independent body of work oriented toward global medical education and cross-cultural scholarship.

Academic Focus and Professional Scope

Her professional activities include classical medical research, clinical education, academic writing, and the translation of specialized Jingfang texts into English. She is actively involved in presenting traditional Chinese medical theory in a form accessible to international scholars, practitioners, and publishers.

Her work emphasizes evidence-based structure, historical accuracy, and clinical applicability, bridging traditional knowledge systems with modern academic standards.

Purpose of This Website

This website serves as a structured presentation of published works, educational contributions, and international academic activities related to Jingfang medicine. All materials are organized to demonstrate sustained professional engagement, scholarly contribution, and original impact within the field.

The site is designed to support academic, publishing, and professional review, including evaluation for EB-1A Extraordinary Ability classification.
