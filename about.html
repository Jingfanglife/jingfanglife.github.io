About Lou Shenshan

Lou Shenshan is a practitioner, researcher, and translator specializing in classical Jingfang (经方) medicine. Her work focuses on academic interpretation, clinical transmission, and the international dissemination of traditional Chinese medical knowledge.

She builds upon the clinical and scholarly legacy of her father, Dr. Lou Shao-kun, a widely recognized authority in Jingfang medicine in China, while developing an independent body of work oriented toward global medical education and cross-cultural scholarship.

Lou Shenshan with her father, Dr. Lou Shao-kun, at the United Nations — representing heritage, lineage, and international academic transmission.

Academic Focus and Professional Scope

Her professional activities include classical medical research, clinical education, scholarly writing, and the translation of specialized Jingfang texts into English.

She is actively engaged in presenting traditional Chinese medical theory in a form accessible to international scholars, practitioners, and publishers, emphasizing evidence-based structure, historical accuracy, and clinical applicability.
