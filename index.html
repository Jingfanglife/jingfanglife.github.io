Jingfang (经方) Medicine · Clinical Education · Publishing & Translation

My work centers on the study, interpretation, and clinical transmission of classical Jingfang (经方) medicine, with an emphasis on evidence-based structure and long-term clinical verification.

Building upon the clinical lineage of my father, Dr. Lou Shao-kun, I focus on presenting classical medical knowledge in a form accessible to international scholars, practitioners, and publishers.
